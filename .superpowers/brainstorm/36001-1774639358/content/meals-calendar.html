Native Patterns Summary

Meals Plan — Native List with Section per day. Tap empty slot → recipe picker sheet. Swipe to remove meal.
Recipes — LazyVGrid with .searchable() modifier. Tap → NavigationLink push to recipe detail.
Calendar — Native calendar grid. Tap day → agenda updates below. Colored sidebar indicators by event type (chore/event/meal).
Scope bar — Native .searchScopes() or segmented Picker for Plan/Recipes toggle.
